## SECTION 3: THE PROBLEM

**Section label:** THE BROKEN MODEL

**Headline:**
Your Creative Team and Your Media Buyer Have Never Spoken.

**Body:**
You're paying one agency to make ads. Another one — or the same one's "other department" — to run them. The brief gets lost in translation. The creative looks great in a deck and dies on day three in the ad account.

Meanwhile, your CAC is climbing. Your ROAS is sliding. And your agency just sent you a monthly report full of impressions and reach instead of the numbers you actually care about.

Sound familiar?

**Pain points (three columns or stacked on mobile):**

🔴 **Creative that doesn't convert**
Beautiful ads. Terrible CAC. Your agency's creative team hasn't looked at your Ads Manager once.

🔴 **Media buyers flying blind**
Your media buyer is optimizing in a vacuum. They didn't make the creative. They can't change the creative. So they adjust the targeting and hope.

🔴 **Reports that say nothing**
Reach. Impressions. Engagement rate. None of these pay your rent. You want CAC, ROAS, and revenue. You keep getting slideshows.

**Case Study 1: BOSS (Boulder Outdoor Survival School)**

BEFORE / AFTER callout: 63 enrollments → 218 enrollments

**Label:** Brand Revival + Performance Media

**The problem:** A respected legacy nonprofit on the brink — outdated brand, fragmented messaging, declining enrollment, no acquisition engine.

**What we built:** Brand refresh and narrative repositioning. Website rebuild with e-commerce. Anthem video + short-form content system. Meta-first performance media with intentional targeting and retargeting.

**What happened:**
- **218 enrollments** on pace for 2026 — best year in company history
- Up from **63 enrollments** in 2024
- **$206 CAC** on a **$6,300 product**
- Instagram: **7,313 → 15,000+** followers

*We didn't run ads. We built a growth system that made BOSS culturally relevant and commercially sustainable.*

---

**Case Study 2: FA × Converse**

BEFORE / AFTER callout: 30 days. 934 pairs sold. 28.78x ROAS.

**Label:** Campaign Launch + Paid Media

**The problem:** Sell 1,000 shoes in 30 days.

**What we built:** Full PDP landing page. 6–12 ads across formats covering the full funnel. Targeted streetwear and skate-adjacent audiences. Off-the-wall storytelling with a drip-fed content release strategy.

**What happened:**
- **119x ROAS** in the first 48 hours
- **934 pairs sold** in 30 days (rest sold the following week)
- **28.78x average ROAS** over the campaign
- **$7.95 cost per purchase** overall
- Cold audience: **361 conversions at $10.29 CPC**, **287,893 impressions**, **2.54% CTR**
- Warm audience: **573 conversions at $6.48 CPC**, **1.69% CTR**
- **1.29M total impressions**, **13,763 link clicks**

*The hype was real. The shoes? Gone.*

**Case Study 3: Middle Calling**

BEFORE / AFTER callout: 0 → 90,000 followers. $0.41 per follow.

**The problem:** Build a political channel from zero. Convert attention into a core audience. Reach people politically homeless — stuck in the middle — and build something they'd actually want to join.

**What we built:** A new social brand with a distinct voice and format. Evergreen anthem creative built for persuasion. A paid system turning stories into scale via Meta distribution. A compounding growth loop: organic velocity + paid amplification.

**What happened:**
- **0 → 90,000 followers** in a few months
- **93rd percentile growth** on Meta
- **$0.41 cost per follow** through paid acquisition
- Currently gaining **~836 followers/day** with minimal paid effort

*We didn't buy followers. We built a message people wanted to join — then used paid to make it inevitable.*
